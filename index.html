A Story About Us

Samata Audio is a government recognised recording studio under Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME), Regd. #UDYAM-WB-18-0021162 . Since 2001, Samata Audio is a sound recording & training studio, conceived from the ground up to offer exceptionally high-quality music recording, Film & video dubbing, Promo or Jingle recording, mixing and mastering, all in a flexibly arranged single studio with a friendly face. We have so many happy clients worldwide those were working with us through internet. Please feel free to contact us for your recording needs. Our recording studio has everything you need for recording, mixing and mastering your tracks. Our professional in-house equipment, isolation booth and sound engineering will make sure you will receive professional quality results with every recording! Our seasoned professionals will work with you to produce the top-quality recordings every time. Come and see for yourself!
